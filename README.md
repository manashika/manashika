## 🌸 About Me

> From solving Business Mathematics problems in school to uncovering insights hidden inside data — my journey into Data Science has always been shaped by curiosity, analytical thinking, and a desire to solve meaningful, real-world problems.

My interest in data grew stronger during my **B.Sc. in Data Science** at M.O.P. Vaishnav College for Women, where I discovered that data is far more than numbers on a spreadsheet — it tells stories, reveals patterns, explains behavior, and helps organizations make better decisions. That realization led me to pursue an **M.Sc. in Data Science at SASTRA University**, where I'm continuing to build my foundation in AI, Machine Learning, Analytics, and intelligent systems.

Along the way, I've gained hands-on experience through internships in **Data Science** and **Web Development** — working with real datasets, preprocessing, exploratory analysis, visualization, predictive modelling, dashboard building, and responsive web interfaces.

I enjoy turning raw, unstructured data into meaningful insight using **Python, SQL, TensorFlow, Scikit-learn, Tableau, and Power BI**. Today, I'm expanding into **Generative AI, LLMs, RAG systems, and AI-powered analytics**, while building projects that connect data, business, and intelligent systems.

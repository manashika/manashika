**My Journey into Data Science**

From solving Business Mathematics problems in school to uncovering the stories hidden inside data, my path into Data Science has been shaped by curiosity, analytical thinking, and a desire to solve meaningful, real-world problems.

That interest grew stronger during my **B.Sc. in Data Science at M.O.P. Vaishnav College for Women**, where I learned that data is far more than numbers in a spreadsheet — it explains behavior, reveals patterns, and helps organizations make better decisions. That realization is what led me to pursue my **M.Sc. in Data Science at SASTRA University**, where I'm continuing to build my foundation in AI, Machine Learning, and Analytics.

Along the way, internships in **Data Science** and **Web Development** gave me hands-on exposure to real-world datasets — preprocessing, exploratory data analysis, visualization, predictive modeling, dashboard creation, and building responsive web interfaces.

I enjoy turning raw, unstructured data into insight using tools like Python, SQL, TensorFlow, Scikit-learn, Tableau, and Power BI. Right now, I'm expanding into **Generative AI, Large Language Models, and AI-powered Analytics**, while continuing to build projects that connect data, business, and intelligent systems.

> *"I don't just analyze data — I explore the stories behind it, transform insights into decisions, and continuously learn to build solutions that create meaningful impact."*
